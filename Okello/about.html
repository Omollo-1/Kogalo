Our Mission

To empower a united, supportive community of Dedan Kimathi University students by fostering leadership, academic excellence, social responsibility, and lifelong camaraderie through meaningful activities and advocacy.

  • Provide academic and personal development support
  • Promote student welfare and advocacy
  • Encourage community service and social involvement
  • Host diverse cultural and social events

Our Vision

To be the premier student association at Dedan Kimathi University, recognized for transforming student life by inspiring growth, responsibility, and impactful leadership, while bridging diverse cultures and backgrounds.

Our History

Established in 2005, DEKUT Comrades Association began as a passionate student initiative to create a supportive student network. Growing from a small group to thousands strong, we have advanced diverse projects ranging from scholarship programs to leadership academies.

Key milestones include the annual Community Outreach Program (2010), Scholarship Initiative (2015), and the Leadership Academy launch (2018), all reflecting our commitment to student growth and community development.
